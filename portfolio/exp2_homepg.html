About Christmas

Christmas is primarily a Christian holiday that celebrates the birth of Jesus Christ. It is observed on December 25th each year. People celebrate Christmas with various traditions and customs that may include decorating Christmas trees, exchanging gifts, attending church services, and sharing festive meals. Gift-giving is a significant part of Christmas, symbolizing the gifts brought to the baby Jesus by the Magi. People exchange presents with family and friends as a way of expressing love and goodwill.
